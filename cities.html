Cities

Visit one of italy's 150 cities

Italy has a large number of cities, and each of them have different characterisitcs. For instance Venice is known for it's canals, and for it's masks, Milan is known for it's fashion, Florence is known for it's art and Maranello is known for it's cars, specifically Ferrari's.

In total, there are 149 cities with over 50,000 residents that are scattered all over the Italian countryside, with many more cities that fall below the 50,000 mark.

Every city has different ways to get to it. Most cities that are in the 149 cities mentioned above has airports that have daily flights from the capitals, such as Rome, Venice, Milan and Florence. Italy also has a wide coverage of railway that reaches many cities. Such cities such as Venice also have access by boat, because the city was not built on land, and busses are also commonly used.
